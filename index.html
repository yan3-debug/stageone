FAQ

Questions before the first stage?

Is StageOne a real working app?

This version is a polished static landing page for GitHub Pages. The product buttons and form are demo-ready, but no backend is connected yet.

How long does it take to find a matching vocalist?

In this demo concept, StageOne can suggest a first shortlist in a few minutes after analyzing your genre, vocal range, mood, BPM, and project goal.

How does StageOne match me with other musicians?

StageOne compares your sound, role, location, availability, and creative direction to recommend singers, producers, writers, and live collaborators who fit your project.

Does the form collect real emails?

No. It only shows a success message in the browser. That keeps the GitHub upload safe and beginner-friendly.
